Specifications §

Battery & range:

  • Battery: 52kWh usable
  • WLTP range (GTS): 226 miles (364 km)
  • Real summer range: 180–210 miles (290–340 km)
  • Real winter range: 100–130 miles (160–210 km)

Charging:

  • Max DC fast charge: 100kW
  • Max AC charge: 7kW (single phase only, not 22kW three phase)
  • Home charge time (7kW): 3–4 hours (10–80%)

Power:

  • GT: 180PS (130kW)
  • GT Performance / GTS: 220PS (160kW)

Dimensions & practicality:

  • Boot: Sufficient for weekend trips
  • Rear legroom: "Really bad" - not a family car
  • Isofix: Can fit baby seat behind driver if driver ≤5'6"

Wheels & tyres:

  • Standard fitment: 19"
  • Tyres: Michelin Pilot Sport 5 (A290-specific variant)
  • 18" wheels compatible (Rallye version, some A110 wheels)
  • Wheel bolt torque: 125Nm
  • Tyre pressure: 33–35psi recommended (not 2.6bar/36psi ECO spec)
  • 5x114.3 PCD, ET30 offset

Paint codes:

  • Alpine Vision Blue: RRR (A290 specific, different from A110's RQR)
  • Deep Black: RPR
  • Nival White: QNC
  • Matte Tornado Grey: KNM
  • Black trim / roof (dual-tone): GNE (same across all trims)

Paint codes are on a sticker on the B pillar (driver's side, visible when door is open).

Wheel notes:

  • OEM wheel paint is soft — scratches easily from normal use
  • Minor marks polish out with a micro-fibre cloth
  • Ceramic coating recommended to protect wheels (CarPro DLUX or similar)
  • Alignment pins (M12×P1.5): useful when refitting wheels to protect calipers. ~£12–13 on Amazon

Trims, packs & standard equipment §

The A290 launched with four trims in the UK. MY26 simplifies the range (see MY26 changes).

MY25 trim levels:

  • GT (180hp / 130kW) — from ~£30,245. 236-mile WLTP range, 0–62 mph in 7.4s, 99 mph top speed.
  • GT Premium (180hp) — adds Nappa leather, Devialet audio, heated steering wheel. Discontinued for MY26.
  • GT Performance (220hp / 160kW) — from ~£34,500. Same power as GTS but cloth seats. Devialet and Telemetrics optional.
  • GTS (220hp / 160kW) — from ~£36,000. 226-mile WLTP range, 0–62 mph in 6.4s, 106 mph top speed.
  • GTS Première Édition (220hp) — limited launch edition. Everything on the GTS plus numbered plate, blue anodised roof rail, French flag on C-pillar, V2G charging capability.

Standard on every A290 (all trims):

  • 52 kWh battery, 100 kW DC fast charging, 11 kW AC charging, heat pump
  • 10.1″ Alpine-Portal touchscreen with Google built-in (Maps, Assistant, Play Store)
  • Wireless Android Auto & Apple CarPlay
  • Arkamys audio system (base; Devialet on GT Premium / GTS)
  • Four Alpine driving modes: Save, Normal, Sport, Personal (adjustable steering, throttle, lighting, Alpine Drive Sound)
  • ESC with full deactivation option
  • Alpine sport chassis
  • Brembo 4-piston monobloc front brake calipers (unpainted; colour options available)
  • Adaptive cruise control (intelligent, with stop & go)
  • Emergency brake assist (AEB)
  • Lane keeping assistant
  • High beam assist
  • Safe distance alert
  • Front, rear and side parking sensors with reversing camera
  • Heated front seats
  • Nappa leather sport steering wheel (flat-bottomed, mid-point)
  • Full LED headlights with four-cross signature DRL
  • 10.3″ digital driver display
  • Wireless smartphone charger
  • Automatic air conditioning
  • Automatic lights & wipers with rain sensor
  • Rear privacy glass
  • Alarm with I-key hands-free
  • ISOFIX front and rear
  • 19″ alloy wheels with Michelin Sport EV tyres

Safety Pack (£300 option on GT/GT Performance; standard on GTS/Première):

  • Blind spot warning with intervention
  • Safe exit warning (alerts when opening door into traffic)
  • Driver attention monitoring
  • Reverse parking warning system (rear cross-traffic alert)
  • Electrochromatic auto-dimming interior mirror

Driving Pack (£700 option on GT/GT Performance; standard on GTS/Première):

  • Hands-free parking system (auto park)
  • Active Driver Assist (enhanced adaptive cruise with steering intervention)
  • Electrochromatic auto-dimming interior mirror (if Safety Pack not fitted)

GTS / Première Édition additions over GT:

  • 220hp (160kW) motor — 300 Nm torque
  • Safety Pack and Driving Pack included as standard
  • Devialet premium audio system with Alpine Drive Sound
  • Alpine Telemetrics Premium (lap timing, performance data logging)
  • Two-tone Nappa leather interior
  • Heated steering wheel
  • Electrically folding exterior mirrors
  • 19″ black Snowflake alloy wheels with Michelin Pilot Sport 5 S tyres
  • Brembo Racing Red calipers (blue optional)

Comparing to a Megane E-Tech? The A290 shares the CMF-BEV platform. Key features the A290 adds: ESC full deactivation, Alpine sport chassis, Brembo brakes, driving modes with Alpine Drive Sound, Telemetrics (GTS). Features common to both: Google infotainment, wireless Android Auto/CarPlay, heat pump, adaptive cruise. The A290 is shorter and sportier; the Megane has more rear space and boot capacity.

Source: Alpine UK Configurator, Alpine UK Brochure (PDF). Prices include £3,750 PiVG grant where applicable.

Charging curve §

  • Peak: 100kW max (77–80kW typical in cold weather is "excellent")
  • Slows significantly above 75% SoC
  • Temperature dependent: 13kW at 2°C vs 66kW at 9°C
  • Cold battery: charges VERY slowly initially
  • Warm battery: 38–66kW at moderate temperatures

Real-world running costs §

Electricity:

  • Overnight charge (20–80%): £1.50–£2.30
  • 10–80%: Under £3
  • Public fast (0–80%): c.£18
  • Ionity with Mobilize pass: £18 (25–100%)
  • 4,000 miles (6,400 km) over 5 months: £185 total

Insurance: £200–300/year typical with NCB. Much cheaper than comparable ICE hot hatches.

Service & maintenance §

  • Regular services can be done at Renault dealers (confirmed)
  • Service plan: £9.99/month mentioned (may vary)
  • Standard manufacturer warranty applies

Common consumables:

  • Tyres: Michelin Pilot Sport 5 (A290-specific variant)
  • Wiper blades: Standard Renault fitment
  • Cabin filter: Related to heater whistle issue

MY26+ changes summary §

  • Full £3,750 grant (vs £1,500 on MY25)
  • One-pedal driving feature (not retrofittable to MY25)
  • White/black roof now no cost option
  • Black now a £700 option
  • APR: 4.9% (vs 2.9% on MY25)
  • Finance c.£20/month increase
  • GT Premium model discontinued
  • Nappa leather and Devialet now only standard on GTS+
