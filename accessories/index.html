Essential first mods §

Key fob cover: Protects the naff plastic key. Allows keyring attachment. For a premium option, LM Parts make hand painted key covers in France, colour matched to your car (Alpine Vision Blue, Deep Black, etc.).

PPF (Paint Protection Film): Highly recommended, especially for matte grey. Full PPF + ceramic: c.£3,800. Critical front panels + side skirts + ceramic: more economical. £150 for critical areas (eBay DIY option). Important: Tell your insurance - some insurers (Aviva) refuse coverage with PPF on young drivers.

Ceramic coating: Recommended for black cars (easier cleaning). Gtechniq mentioned specifically.

Home chargers (confirmed working) §

  • Hypervolt 3: Works well (tethered recommended)
  • Ohme ePod (untethered): £900–1k+ installed via independent. Uses car's own cable. Octopus integration works with workaround
  • Zappi: Works with IOG for some ("2 A290s here, both charge perfectly since July")
  • Andersen Quartz (7kW): Works well, get surge protection
  • Simpson & Partners 7kW freestanding: Works fine
  • Project EV Apex: Tethered option

Avoid: Over-integrated setups (IOG + specific charger + car all talking = more likely to fail).

The 3-pin granny charger works as a top up/temporary solution before a proper charger is installed. Came with the car.

Dashcams §

Blackvue 4K Front & Rear: DIY fitted using fuse taps in glove box. "Cable routing easy in headliner and into rear hatch." Much better value than the optional Kenwood (c.£2k).

Other options: Nextbase 622GW + rear camera (hardwired to fuse box). The fuse box in the glove box makes hardwiring easy.

Cup holders & bottle holders (the eternal struggle) §

The A290 famously doesn't come with a cup or bottle holder. It's the single most discussed missing feature. Here are the solutions owners have found:

Purpose built options:

  • Pure Plastics A290 Cup Holder: Custom fit for the A290, available standalone or bundled with a Yeti Rambler cup. Bristol based, ships nationally. pure-plastics.co.uk
  • Idle Hands (PistonHeads): 3D printed in carbon fibre, black, or blue. Two designs - centre console (over phone holder) or inside armrest bin. Also makes armrest trays (3 sizes) with Apple Wallet/card slots. Files on Cults3D if you have your own printer.

Generic options that work:

  • Clip-in centre console holders (Amazon, various)
  • Air vent mounted cup holders - used by multiple owners
  • Door pocket bottle wedges

R5 parts: The Renault 5 "baguette holder" fits the A290 and can hold a bottle.

If you've found another solution, share it in the owner communities and we'll add it here.

Wheels, spacers & tyres §

Wheel spacers: 15mm rear spacers popular. 20mm spacers used with lowering. Direnza spacers didn't fit (not machined deep enough for centre spigot) - check spigot depth before buying.

Lowering: H&R springs popular. 30mm lowering kits available. Life110 developing options (previous A110 specialist). AC13 Premier can help. Warning: UK road conditions make this painful for commuting.

Winter wheels: 18" option available (Rallye spec, some A110 wheels compatible). 225/45 R18 fitment. ET30 offset, 5x114.3 PCD. A290 brakes/axle from A110, not R5.

Tyres:

  • Michelin Cross Climate 3: excellent in rain/snow
  • Michelin Pilot Alpin 5: A290-specific winter tyre available
  • Costco: £183+VAT for 4 (November £100 off deal)
  • Pirelli Cinturato: all weather alternative

Styling & accessories §

From Alpine/dealers:

  • Blue wheel centre caps (if not installed, may be in glove box)
  • French flag C-pillar emblems (factory option or parts department)
  • Blue steering wheel logo (requires new airbag, £275 inc VAT + fitting)
  • Indoor car cover (Xray) from Alpine accessories
  • Floor mats included on GTS

Aftermarket:

  • Door light projectors (rechargeable or wired, colour changeable)
  • HEKO wind deflectors (£40 eBay, for R5/5E fitment)
  • Rati Frunk - 10L storage under the bonnet, no tools needed, rubberized spacers for safe install
  • Frunk.AT - "excellent build quality, clear instructions"
  • Rear spoiler extension (KTT Design)
  • Steering lock: Stoplock Pro Elite or Disklok recommended

Specialist suppliers §

  • AC13 Premier: Lowering, spacers, wheels, wraps, window tints. 19" Rotiform wheels + aero discs. Multiple A290 builds completed.
  • LM Parts (France): Hand painted key covers colour matched to your car. 35+ colours available. UK delivery. Use code LMP15 for 15% off two or more.
  • Rati: 10L frunk for A290/R5. No-tools install, rubberized spacers.
  • Frunk.AT: Alternative frunk option. Excellent build quality reported by owners.
  • New Look Detailing: PPF & ceramic coatings. Multiple A290s completed.
  • Life110: Geo settings for A290. Previously A110 specialist. Developing lowering solutions.

Parts compatibility (R5 & A110) §

R5 parts that fit:

  • Rubber floor mats (LHD only officially, but fit)
  • Reversible boot liner
  • Frunk (same fitment)
  • Battery indicator box (may be retrofittable)

A110 parts:

  • 18" rear wheels may fit (fronts narrower, can't buy full set)
  • Brakes/axle shared with A110
  • Geo settings similar
